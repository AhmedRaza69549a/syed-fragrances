Shipping & Delivery

At Syed Fragrances, we ensure our high-concentration impressions reach your doorstep through reliable and trusted courier partners across Pakistan.

Processing & Transit Times

Orders are typically prepared and dispatched within 1-2 business days. For deliveries within Karachi, you can expect your package to arrive within 2-5 business days. Timelines for other regions may vary based on the specific destination.

Shipment Tracking

Once your order is handed over to the courier service, we are no longer liable for delays caused by the transit provider. However, we remain committed to assisting you if any logistical issues arise during the journey of your package.

Support

If you have concerns regarding your delivery, our customer service team is ready to assist to ensure your experience remains seamless and satisfying. Contact us via WhatsApp for immediate inquiries.
